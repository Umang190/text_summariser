How It Works

This summarizer uses a machine learning model called BART (Bidirectional and Auto-Regressive Transformers) to generate meaningful summaries from long pieces of text.

It's been trained on huge text datasets to understand language patterns. Just paste your content below, and click the button to get a quick summary!
